The problem

The family called. Nobody called back. Another provider won the client.

In most domiciliary agencies, the same person handles enquiries, scheduling, recruitment and on-call. Enquiries don't get lost on purpose — they get lost because everyone is busy and nobody owns the follow-up.

The call after 5pm

Goes to voicemail. The family is already ringing the next provider on their list.

The web form

Lands in an inbox, gets half-read between visits, and quietly drops down the screen.

The referral

Accepted verbally, never logged. Two days later nobody's sure if anyone called back.

The follow-up

Loses every time to today's rota gap. Important always loses to urgent.
